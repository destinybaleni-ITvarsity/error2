INSECT HOUSE

The Insect House is an immersive indoor experience, where the intricate world of insects is revealed. From exotic beetles to colourful butterflies, visitors can get up close and personal with diverse array of fascinating creatures.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

From the main entrance, take the path leading towards the "Exhibits" area. The Insect House is on the left side of the path.
