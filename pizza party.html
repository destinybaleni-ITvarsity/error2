PIZZA SHOP

When hunger strikes during your zoo excursion, refuel at our "Pizza Party" and savor a slice of cheesy, gooey goodness. Our fresh, mouthwatering pizzas will satisfy your cravings and give you the energy to continue exploring all the exciting attractions the zoo has to offer.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

The Pizza Shop is situated near the Monkey Trail. Continue on the path and turn left when you see the "Food and Beverages" sign.
